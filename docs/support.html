Frequently Asked Questions

Is FurēPET free to use?

FurēPET is free to download. Some premium features are available through an optional subscription.

How do I add my pet's health records?

Once you create an account and add a pet profile, you can upload vet records, log medications, and track weight directly from the app dashboard.

Is my pet's data secure?

Yes. Your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties. We take your privacy seriously.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from within the app under Settings. If you need assistance, contact us at hello@furepet.com and we'll take care of it.

What devices are supported?

FurēPET is available for iPhone and iPad running iOS 16 or later.
